Company Profile


Chemdah LLC has set out to develop a human capital that meets the market demands, to inculcate the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship into individuals,to create jobs and to provide excellent management services.

The Conglomerate of the future.



Our Vision

Our vision is to be one of the leading organizations in training individuals and institutions to achieve their dreams and goals as well as to provide high quality services

The 2 tenets of our corporate vision are:

Youth Empowerment: to help young people to hone relevant skills that will benefit their lives and impact society for the better

Job Creation & Job Satisfaction: to employ as many young people as possible and to help people love or upgrade their work. This is aimed at reducing the unemployment rate of Ghana and improving livelihoods; thereby boosting the Ghanaian economy.



Our Mission

Our mission is to guide individuals and institutions to actualize their dreams through consultancy services and eductional seminars and workshops as well as creating small and medium scale businesses.
